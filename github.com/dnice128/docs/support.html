Common Questions

How do I sync my data across devices?

Make sure iCloud is enabled on all your devices and that you're signed in with the same Apple ID. Becoming syncs automatically via iCloud.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Go to Profile → Manage Data and choose Export as JSON or Export as CSV.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile → Manage Data. You can clear all history or delete all habits. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

I lost my data after reinstalling

If you had iCloud sync enabled, your data should restore automatically after reinstalling. Make sure you're signed in to the same iCloud account and give it a few minutes to sync.
